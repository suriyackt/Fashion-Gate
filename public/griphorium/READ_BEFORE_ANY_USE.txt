Griphorium is a fast handwriting script with a clear sense of movement. It feels written in one go, where the hand rarely slows down and each letter follows the next with natural momentum.

The shapes are slightly sharp and energetic, avoiding softness in favor of precision and flow. Connections are quick and confident, keeping small irregularities that make the writing feel human and unforced rather than carefully styled.

Designed by Mans Greback, Griphorium focuses on the moment of writing itself. It reflects the character of notes, names, and short lines written with intent, capturing the rhythm of handwriting that values speed, clarity, and personal expression.

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers.
